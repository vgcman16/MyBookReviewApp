Book Review App Preview

Search Tab

  • Search bar for books
    Users can search for books by title, author, or ISBN
  • List of search results
    Displays book cover, title, author, and average rating
  • Book details view
    Shows full book information, user reviews, and reading progress

Reading List Tab

  • List of books in reading list
    Displays books added by the user for future reading
  • Option to remove books from list
    Users can easily manage their reading list

Recommendations Tab

  • List of recommended books
    Personalized book suggestions based on user's reading history and preferences
  • Option to add books to reading list
    Users can quickly add interesting recommendations to their reading list

Profile Tab

  • User information
    Displays username, email, and other account details
  • Favorite books
    Shows a list of books marked as favorites by the user
  • Reading statistics
    Provides insights on reading habits, books completed, and time spent reading
  • Logout option
    Allows users to securely log out of their account

Additional Features

  • Barcode scanner for adding books
    Users can quickly add books to their lists by scanning the ISBN barcode
  • Reading progress tracker
    Allows users to update and view their progress for each book
  • Book reviews and ratings
    Users can write reviews and rate books they've read
  • Social sharing of books
    Enables users to share interesting books on social media platforms
